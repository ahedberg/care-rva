Adoption Policies

Our adoption policies may vary based upon the dog's individual needs. However, we always prefer homes with, and may require, a constructed fenced yard. We ask for, and strongly consider, a veterinary reference. Your vet can tell us that you are a responsible pet owner - that you keep your animals' vaccines and heartworm prevention up-to-date, have them spayed or neutered, and maintain their health as recommended. We do not adopt to persons under 21 years of age, in homeless situations, or to those who are not totally self-sufficient. For small dogs and puppies, we require homes without larger animals or preschool aged children. Puppies are adopted to homes with adults who are home most of each day for socialization and training. A home visit is required for all adoptions. Our goal is to find responsible pet owners with safe environments for their families and animals. We reserve the right to refuse an adoption based upon any factor we deem as unsatisfactory for our rescued animals.

Donate to C.A.R.E.

C.A.R.E. is a 501c3 rescue raising funds to support our unadoptable sanctuary dogs and offset veterinary expenses. Donations are accepted via PayPal or at our adoption stands. We also raise funds through an online auction.
