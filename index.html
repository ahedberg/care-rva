Capital Area Rescue Effort

C.A.R.E. was founded in 2011 in the Richmond, VA area. We rescue dogs from abuse and neglect situations and work to pair them with loving, permanent homes through a thorough and rigorous adoption procedure.

Our Rescues

We use Petfinder to keep our listings of available dogs up-to-date. We rescue dogs of all breeds. Find your new best friend today!

Donate to C.A.R.E.

C.A.R.E. is a 501c3 rescue raising funds to support our unadoptable sanctuary dogs and offset veterinary expenses. Donations are accepted via PayPal or at our adoption stands.

Other ways to donate:

  • Shop on our eBay store.
  • List C.A.R.E. as your beneficiary on AmazonSmile.
  • Link your Kroger Plus card to C.A.R.E. We are organization number 81838.
  • Purchase something from our Amazon wish list.
  • Volunteer in our kennel. Follow our Facebook page for volunteer training dates!
