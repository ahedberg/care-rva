Adoption Policies

Basics

  • We ask for, and strongly consider, a veterinary reference. We specifically look for the following:
    • You keep your animals' vaccines and heartworm prevention up-to-date.
    • Your spay or neuter your animals.
    • You maintain your animals' health as recommended.
  • We do not adopt to persons under 21 years of age, in homeless situations, or to those who are not totally self-sufficient.
  • A home visit is required for all adoptions.
  • We reserve the right to refuse an adoption based upon any factor we deem as unsatisfactory for our rescued animals.
  • Our adoption policies may vary based upon the dog's individual needs.

Fences and Homes

  • We always prefer homes with a constructed fenced yard.
  • We may require a constructed fenced yard for some dogs. The dog's Petfinder page will specify fence requirements.
  • We require that our dogs be placed in nonsmoking homes.

Puppies

  • We consider our puppies to be dogs of less than 12 months of age.
  • We do not place our puppies in homes where families are gone 40+ hours per week.
  • We do not place puppies in homes with larger dogs.
  • We do not place puppies in homes with preschool-aged children for the safety of the puppy and the child.
